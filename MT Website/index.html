WELCOME

Here at Motor Technik, we're passionate about BMWs and have built a strong reputation within our local community. Whether you own a brand new model or a classic car, our experienced technicians will be able to service and maintain your BMW to the highest standard.

With a 5 bay workshop, trained technicians and the latest diagnostic and tyre equipment, we are able to offer complete service to you under one roof.

Our helpful and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to discuss your requirements for all things BMW related and are able to draw on our many years of experience.

We pride ourselves on a professional yet friendly service at a huge saving on main dealer prices.
